Uncertainty-Aware Incremental Automatic Modulation Classification with Bayesian Neural Network
IEEE Internet of Things Journal
Modulation classification · Bayesian methods · Uncertainty

Proposed a Bayesian learning framework for uncertainty-aware incremental automatic modulation classification. The approach first optimizes the model via a cumulative loss in a frequentist manner, then applies Laplace approximation and variational inference to the final layers for statistical inference. This yields robust calibration and scalability, allowing the classifier to refine its predictions as more signal samples arrive.

GNN-based Meta-Learning for Adaptive Power Control in Dynamic D2D Communications
IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology
Meta-learning · Graph neural networks · Power control

Designed an environment-adaptive power control method for dynamic D2D networks where network size, rate requirements, and deployment area change over time. By combining graph neural networks with meta-learning, the method improves both scalability and adaptation speed compared with conventional GNN-based power control, achieving higher average sum-throughput and faster convergence in new environments.
